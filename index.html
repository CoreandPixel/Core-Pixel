About Core & Pixel

Founded in 2024 by two visionary friends, Core & Pixel is an unregistered startup on a mission to revolutionize how businesses solve their most complex technology challenges. What began as a passion project has evolved into a comprehensive tech solutions provider dedicated to bridging the gap between business problems and elegant technical implementations.

Our Story

Born from the frustration of seeing businesses struggle with outdated technology infrastructure and poor user experiences, we decided to take action. Armed with years of collective experience in software architecture, product design, and cloud systems, we created Core & Pixel to be the tech partner businesses didn't know they needed.

What We Do

We provide end-to-end technology solutions across the entire software development lifecycle. From initial architecture design to deployment and optimization, we handle every aspect of your tech stack with meticulous attention to detail and a focus on scalability.

Our Specializations

Web Development: We build modern, responsive web applications using cutting-edge frameworks like React, Next.js, and Vue. Every pixel is crafted with purpose, combining beautiful design with bulletproof functionality.

Cloud Infrastructure: We specialize in architecting and managing robust cloud solutions on AWS, Azure, and GCP. Our expertise in auto-scaling, disaster recovery, and cost optimization ensures your infrastructure works as hard as you do.

Our Approach

We believe the best solutions come from understanding both the business problem and the technical landscape. We don't just buildwe partner with you to create systems that drive real business value. Our cross-functional expertise spans system architecture, backend development, frontend engineering, UX research, and data infrastructure.

Why Core & Pixel

In a world of one-trick ponies, we're your full-spectrum tech partner. Whether you need a complete system redesign, cloud migration, new web platform, or data infrastructure, we bring the same level of dedication and excellence to every project. We're small enough to care about details, experienced enough to handle complexity.
