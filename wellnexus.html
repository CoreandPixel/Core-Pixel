WellNexus

A unified healthcare ecosystem connecting patients, doctors, and pharmacies for a seamless medical experience.

01. The Problem

Managing healthcare is a surprisingly fragmented and stressful experience. The current digital ecosystem forces patients and caregivers to navigate a maze of disconnected tools, creating inefficiency and the risk of dangerous medical errors.

  • Fragmented System: Juggling multiple apps for records and appointments.
  • Chronic Inefficiency: Long waits and administrative hurdles.
  • Risk of Errors: Gaps in care due to poor data tracking.

03. The Solution

A centralized hub for all healthcare needs.

Unified Dashboard

Bring appointments, messages, and records into one place.

Effortless Booking

Find, book, and manage appointments in seconds.

Simplified Pharmacy

Order refills and manage prescriptions with a single tap.

Secure Access

Provide peace of mind with centralized, secure health data.

Real-World Scenarios

Our research allowed us to define key user stories. These guided the design of our core features to ensure we solved tangible, real-world problems.

Booking a Specialist

Persona: Priya

Instantly filter specialists, view availability, and book a time slot during a lunch break.

Refilling a Prescription

Persona: Alex

Receive low-medication alerts and request a refill for delivery with a single tap.

Accessing Results

Persona: Alex

View blood test numbers and read simplified, jargon-free explanations of results.
